# V2.6 Norm Interaction Safety Rules

1. Interaction is not outcome.
2. Override is not automatic victory.
3. Conflict is not rebellion.
4. Exception is not permission for harm.
5. Support is not automatic compliance.
6. Supersession is not deletion.
7. Duplicate is not silent merge.
8. Proceduralization is not meeting completion.
9. Legitimization is not direct legitimacy mutation.
10. Contestation is not punishment or rebellion.
11. High-risk commands cannot become valid exceptions.
12. Player-visible reports cannot expose hidden interaction internals.

Additional rule:

Norm interactions may only preview bounded future effects.  
They may not directly mutate trust, legitimacy, authority, obedience, resources, task completion, meeting completion, building creation, or actor removal.
